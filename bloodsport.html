Bloodsport Battlebots CFD Simulation

The Battlebots team Bloodsport reach out to me and asked for ways to reduce the aerodynamic loading on their weapon motor when their weapon blade was spinning. I ran a CFD sim on the original set up and found that the loading from the drag was a very similar number to the loading reported by the team's data. I then put a chamfer on the sides of the blades and ran the sim again and this was able to reduce the loading by about half with a solution that should be easy to manufacture.
