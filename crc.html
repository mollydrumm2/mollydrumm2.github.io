Combat Robotics @ Cornell

CRC builds robots! I was a part of the Sportsman subteam of CRC, whose goal is to build a 12 lb robot every year to compete at the Norwalk Havoc Robot League (NHRL) competition in the spring. The catch, however, is that Sportsman aims to use a different weapon strategy than high speed spinners, and instead employs lifters, flippers, and other configurations to destroy the competition. We design the robot in the fall, speccing motors and parts and using Autodesk Fusion to make sure it's all under the 12 lb weight limit. Then, we manufacture parts and build the bot in the spring, working to get it all done before competition. In my Master's year, I was no longer on Sportsman and instead worked on the Marketing subteam to promote the team.
